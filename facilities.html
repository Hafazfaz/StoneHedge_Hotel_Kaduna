AHMADU BELLO HALL

Located on the first floor and can accommodate up to 250 people for conferencing and 120 people for a function. The room is fully air-conditioned and equipped with state of the art equipment. It also has a private holding area for VIP’s to relax before the conference / function.

NNAMDI AZIKIWE HALL

Located on the ground floor and can accommodate up to 100 people for conferencing or a small intimate function for up to 60 people. This hall is also fully air-conditioned and equipped with state of the art equipment.

OBAFEMI AWOLOWO BOARD ROOM

This is a fully equipped and air-conditioned board room for 18 people for all executive meetings. The board room can also be converted into a conference room for 50 people.
